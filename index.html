About Boranex

Engineering Excellence in Demanding Environments

Boranex Middle East LLC is a premier engineering solutions provider serving the oil & gas, water, and electricity sectors across Iraq and the wider region since 1998. Under the leadership of our CEO and General Manager, we deliver comprehensive project support—from supply, installation, commissioning, training, warranty, and ongoing maintenance with unparalleled technical support tailored to meet the most demanding industry requirements.

As the exclusive dealer in Iraq for numerous recognized industrial brands, we bring world-class equipment and solutions to our clients, ensuring reliability, efficiency, and performance in challenging operational environments.

Our extensive network of partnerships with leading manufacturers allows us to provide diverse, high-quality solutions that address the unique challenges faced by our clients across multiple sectors. We take pride in our position as Iraq's Premium Lift Solutions Provider, building on our decades of experience and commitment to excellence.

Products

Industry-leading lifting and material handling solutions

Grove Mobile Cranes

All Terrain

Grove All-Terrain Cranes combine exceptional mobility with impressive lifting capacities ranging from 60 to 400 tons, featuring models like the GMK5250L, GMK3060, and GMK6400 that excel in Iraq's diverse work environments.

With advanced MEGATRAK® suspension, TWIN-LOCK™ boom pinning system, and MAXbase variable outrigger positioning, these cranes deliver superior performance on rugged construction sites while maintaining the highway speeds needed for rapid deployment across projects throughout the region.

Rough Terrain

Grove Rough Terrain cranes feature rugged deep box section frames specifically engineered to handle Iraq's harshest work environments, with the GRT8100 (100 ton), RT530E-2 (30 ton), and RT9130E-2 (130 ton) models offering industry-leading capabilities.

With full-vision cabs, advanced load moment indicators, and four-wheel drive/four-wheel steer configurations, these RT cranes deliver exceptional maneuverability and lifting precision in remote locations, oil fields, and unprepared terrain where conventional cranes cannot operate effectively.

Grove Carrydeck

Grove Carrydeck cranes including the GCD15 (13.6 ton), GCD20 (20 ton), and GCD25 (25 ton) models deliver exceptional lifting capability in a compact, maneuverable package ideal for industrial facilities and confined workspaces throughout Iraq.

With 360-degree rotation, pick-and-carry functionality, and optional material handling attachments, these industrial cranes excel in plant maintenance, equipment installation, and specialized material handling operations where space constraints prohibit the use of larger equipment while still requiring precision lifting.

Grove Truck Mounted

Grove Truck Cranes including the TMS700E (50-55 ton), TMS800E (70 ton), and TMS9000-2 (100 ton) models combine rapid mobility with impressive lifting capabilities ideal for urban infrastructure projects and construction sites across Iraq.

Featuring quick setup systems, long-reach booms, and the industry's most advanced operators' cab with CCS (Crane Control System), these truck-mounted cranes balance exceptional reach with precision control, making them the preferred solution for technical lifts in congested areas where both mobility and lifting power are essential.

National Boom Trucks

National Crane currently produces 12 series of boom trucks all which are reliable, durable and maintain an ease of operations. These products are perfect for demanding work routines.

Featuring telescopic boom technology, multi-position outriggers, and advanced load management systems, National boom trucks excel in construction, utility work, and material delivery applications where both lifting power and precise placement are critical requirements.

With models ranging from 9.1 ton to 27.2 ton capacity, National provides the ideal boom truck solution for every project need, combining the mobility of commercial chassis with exceptional lifting capabilities for the most demanding job sites.

Manitowoc Crawler Cranes

Manitowoc began producing lattice boom crawler cranes at its facility in Manitowoc in 1925. It has led the industry in sales, innovation, performance and support.

With models including the 8500-1 (80 ton), 11000-1 (100 ton), MLC100-1 (100 ton), and 12000-1 (110 ton), these powerful crawler cranes deliver exceptional lifting capacity with precision control systems that ensure millimeter-accurate lifts even in challenging environments.

Featuring advanced load management technology and robust undercarriages designed for optimal ground bearing pressure, Manitowoc crawler cranes excel in bridge construction, foundation work, power plant construction, and heavy industrial applications where stability and lifting precision are paramount.
